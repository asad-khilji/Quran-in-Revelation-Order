1. Surah Alaq Ayah 1-5

Introduction

When the Prophet was 40 years old he kept on seeing good righteous dreams. Then the Prophet would see the dream come true when he woke up. Then a love of being alone was endowed into the Prophet.

The Cave of Hira

The Prophet was by himself in the cave of hira. The Prophet wanted to get away from the heavy sins of Makkah. The Prophet followed the tradition of Ibrahim as he sought solitude in the cave searching for the truth. The Prophet spent a number of nights in the cave then he would go back to the cave after taking some provisions. Then Khadija would bring food and water directly to the Prophet without him asking her. Then the Quran and the truth came to the Prophet while he was in the cave during Ramadhan on one of the last 10 odd nights.

Jibreel and the Prophet

An angel, came to the Prophet and grabbed him tightly until he could not breather then he let him and go and said Read! or follow along with me. The Prophet said I cannot read nor have I ever read anything. Then the angel grabbed him tightly until he could not breathe and then he let him go and the angel said read. The Prophet said I cannot read. This is like the angel is tranferrring the knowledge to the Prophet heart to heart. Then the angel grabbed him tightly until he could not breathe and he let him go and he said Read in the name of your Master who created.

The First Revelation

The angel said Read in the name of your Master who created. He created man from a thing that hangs. Read while your Master is noble. Who taught with the pen. He taught man what he did not know. These five ayat were the first to be revealed. After it was revealed the Prophet rushed down the mountain. As the Prophet was coming down he saw the angel in its true form that covered the whole sky. The angel said Muhammad I am Jibreel and you Muhammad are the Messenger of God. Then the Prophet rushed home to Khadija.

Khadija comforts the Prophet

The Prophet got home and said to Khadija to cover him. Khadija covered him with a blanket. After a while the Prophet calmed down. The Prophet tells Khadija everything. He feels terrifed and does not know what happened. Khadija consoles the Prophet telling him that Allah would never humiliate him because he would take care of the orphan. The Prophet would keeps ties with his family. The Prophet would carry the worries of those who had no one.

Waraqa bin Nawfal bin Asad

Khadija takes the Prophet to her cousin, Waraqa. The Prophet tells Waraqa what he saw and heard. Waraqa says that was Namuse or the one that carries a secret of the heavens. The one that was sent to Musa before. Waraqa wished he could join the Prophet and support when his people would kick him out of his city. The Prophet was shocked that his people would drive him away. This is the reality with all the Prophets. Then Waraqa died shortly afterwards.

The Long Gap of Revelation

There is a long gap of revelation. The revelation stopped coming down. During this time Allah would send Jibreel and Israfil took accompany the Prophet. Then after a long pause of revelation Surah Qalam was revealed.
